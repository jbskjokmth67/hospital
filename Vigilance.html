Vigilance

In-charge Information

Dr. Rabi Narayan Sahu

Chief Vigilance officer (I/C)

Professor & HoD, Dept. of Neuro Surgery

AIIMS-Bhubaneswar.

Email: cvo@aiimsbhubaneswar.edu.in

 The Chief Vigilance Officer advises the Institute and Hospital administration on Vigilance complaints concerning the employees and academics in order to enable and ensure integrity. The CVO receives and investigates vigilance related complaints, sensitise the institute community against matters related to corruption, streamlining procedures, processes and encourage a culture of integrity on the Hospital and Institute.

“If you have any information on corruption in this institute or if you are a victim of corruption you may complain cvo@aiimsbhubaneswar.edu.in.
