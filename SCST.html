AIIMS Bhubaneswar

There will be occasions when our services will not be up to your expectations.

Please do not hesitate to register your complaints. It will only help to serve you better.

A Complaint Register is kept in the Office of the Registrar for lodging your Complaints. An institute Mail lD (scstcell@aiimsbhubaneswar.edu.in) has also been opened where also the reports can be sent. Every complaint will be duly acknowledged and sincere attempt will be made to solve your problem.
